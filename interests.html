My field of interests


Archaeology

Archaeology is a fascinating field that allows us to uncover the mysteries of our past by studying artifacts and physical remains left behind by our ancestors. One of the most interesting aspects of archaeology is that it allows us to piece together a detailed picture of how people lived in the past, from their daily routines to their beliefs, traditions, and social structures.

Technology

Smartphone technology has revolutionized the way we communicate, work, and live our daily lives. One interesting aspect of smartphone tech is how it has evolved over time to become a powerful and versatile tool that fits in the palm of our hands. One of the most notable advancements in smartphone technology is the development of high-quality cameras.

Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field at the centre of which is a 22-yard (20-metre) pitch with a wicket at each end, each comprising two bails balanced on three stumps.
